Neue Frutiger World

My typeface of choice is Neue Frutiger World, sourced from the Monotype Type Foundry, designed and directed by Akira Kobayashi, who had his start in designing Japanese fonts. Neue Frutiger World is a humanist, sans serif typeface, designed with readability, language diversity, and UX accessibility in mind.

Neue Frutiger World is based on the original "Frutiger" typeface which was designed by Adrian Frutiger. The type face is meant to embody all that is identifiable with the original, clarity and warmth, while also expanding the global version to 10 weights and designing the typeface in 250 languages, including Latin, Greek, Cyrillic, Georgian, Armenian, Hebrew, Arabic, Thai and Vietnamese.

Overall the invention of this font was was intended to expand different font weights in bolds to global consumers who otherwise may not have had access to such weights in their language. The typeface is strongly associated with corporate advertising, print, signage, and UX aesthetics in mind for a pleasant user experience on a global scale. Typefaces like these are enjoyed among youthful subcultures and aesthetic retrospective trends like "Frutiger Aero." For a largely corporate associated typeface, it is appreciated for refreshing sensibility and accessibility!
